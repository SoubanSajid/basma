    About Us

    Get Ready for Success with Basma Your Path to Growth.

    "Basma" is a Dubai-based management and professional development institute, accredited and authorized by the Knowledge and Human Development Authority (KHDA) in Dubai. Basma is also a member of Dubai SME and has many partnerships with local and international academic and training institutions, such as Meta Airs International American University, the American Academic Board for Education and Training in Washington, and Canada Global Center for Consulting and Training (CGC) in Canada.

    Our Activities

    Shaping Growth Through Learning

    Education and training are key pillars for economic and human development. Through our programs, we prepare individuals and raise efficiency to achieve the highest levels of performance. Basma provides a range of activities, including:

    Management and professional development training

    Basma trains and develops human resources in the fields of management and leadership according to the latest accredited training programs aimed at empowering its customers and increasing their competitiveness.

    Technical Skills Training

    Basma offers training programs at the highest levels in the field of media production, where the trainee gains knowledge and experience to produce using the latest technology.

    Fine Arts Training

    Basma offers diverse courses across artistic disciplines—workshops for all age groups focus on both traditional and modern handicrafts, nurturing creative expression.

    Languages

    The institute teaches multiple languages using international standards, with tailored programs for general proficiency and business-specific communication.

    Educational Services

    Basma provides preparation for exams like EmSAT, IELTS, TOEFL, and TOEIC. Options include group sessions and children's courses, offering flexibility for various learners.
